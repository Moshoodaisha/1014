About me

Akintayo Olushola

Product Design Lead · Interswitch Group
Background

Throughout my career, I have built a reputation for dedication, thoroughness, big-picture thinking, and attention to detail. These qualities are a manifestation of my desire to create a positive impact and to do great work for my customers, my company, and myself.

I'm a Product Design Lead based in Lagos, Nigeria, with 7+ years of experience shaping digital products. My work sits at the intersection of systems thinking and hands-on craft from defining design strategy and building scalable component libraries, to getting into the details of interactions that make products feel intuitive and trustworthy.

What I do

I take complex, fragmented product ecosystems and bring them into coherence. That means establishing shared design languages, leading cross-functional teams, and designing experiences that hold up under real-world pressure I build things that last and scale..

Beyond the screen

When I'm not designing, I'm thinking about the business logic behind why products succeed or fail. I'm drawn to the messy, under-designed parts of industries the back-office tools, the merchant dashboards, the operator interfaces where good design genuinely moves the needle.
